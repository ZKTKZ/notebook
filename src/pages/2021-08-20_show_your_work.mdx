# Show Your Work

![Book cover of title](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/I/71MTgEEjNVL.jpg) 

I do fun stuff every day.
I also learn stuff every day.

This is a tremendous opportunity.
If I share what I'm doing at the moment in an interesting way, I can eventually [make money](https://genius.com/Dc26-im-not-a-businessman-im-a-business-man-annotated) [^1] while doing stuff I'd have done anyway!

Process over product [^2]

[^2] This is from Austin Kleon's book, [Show Your Work](https://austinkleon.com/show-your-work). It's a light but worthwhile read. Or so I'm told.
